# Business Insights

## 1. Call Answer Rate
The dashboard shows a high call answer rate, indicating that most incoming calls are successfully handled by agents.
However, unanswered calls still exist and may represent missed opportunities to assist customers.
**Recommendation**:
Monitor peak call hours and adjust staffing levels accordingly.

## 2. Resolution Rate
Resolution rate is lower than answer rate, suggesting that not all answered calls are fully resolved.
**Recommendation:**
Investigate escalation processes and provide agents with additional resources to improve first-call resolution.

## 3. Agent Performance
Agent performance varies across key metrics including total calls handled, average speed of answer, and customer satisfaction.
**Recommendation:**
Identify top-performing agents and use their workflows as benchmarks for training other team members.

## 4. Customer Satisfaction
Average satisfaction remains moderate, indicating opportunities to improve service quality.

**Recommendation:**
Improve call handling procedures and provide agents with better support tools.
